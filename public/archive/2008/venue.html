RubyNation Venue

RubyNation will be held at the Center of Innovative Technology's conference facility in Herndon, Virginia, which is near the Washington Dulles International Airport. Most of the conference will be held in the large auditorium just behind the main registration desk.

IMPORTANT: You will need to bring an ID to get into the building.

The CIT Building is a landmark in Northern Virginia. It has a futuristic, "upside-down" shape, and can be seen and recognized from miles away. You will know it when you see it.

The address for the CIT is:

CIT Complex 2214 Rock Hill Road Herndon, Virginia 20170
