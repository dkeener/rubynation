About RubyNation

RubyNation is an annual Ruby conference serving the Virginia, West Virginia, Maryland, and Washington, DC areas.

This year's RubyNation will happen on June 11 - 13, 2009 in Reston, VA.

Why RubyNation?

  • Bring Ruby rock stars to Washington.
  • Develop our local talent. Want to become a Ruby leader? Come and share your views.
  • Provide a networking forum. Need a Ruby job? Need some help with a Ruby project?
  • Provide a Ruby business forum. Are you hiring? Have a Ruby product or service to market?
  • Provide a Ruby education forum. Want to hear what the Ruby leaders think? Do you want to learn what they know?
  • Enhance your career propsects. It's a tough economy out there. Beef up your skill set and your marketability.

Still Not Convinced?

Last year, RubyNation sold out in record time! This year, we're holding RubyNation in a bigger venue with even more great speakers than last year.

Check out our archive if you'd like to see what last year's conference looked like. We're sure you'll like what you see!
